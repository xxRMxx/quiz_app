How to play BlackJack Quiz?
🎯
Goal: Get close to 21 points

Try to accumulate as close to 21 total points as possible across all 5 questions.

📊
Scoring System

Points = |Your Answer - Correct Answer|. Lower points are better!

💥
Bust Rule

If you reach 22+ total points, you're BUSTED and eliminated from the round.

🏆
Winning

Player closest to 21 points without busting wins the game!

Example:
Question: "How many legs does a spider have?"
Correct Answer: 8
You answer 6 → 2 points (|6-8| = 2)
You answer 10 → 2 points (|10-8| = 2)
You answer 8 → 0 points (perfect!)
