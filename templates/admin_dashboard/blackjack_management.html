How BlackJack Quiz Works

  • 5 Questions: Each quiz contains exactly 5 numerical questions
  • Points System: Points = how far off your answer is from the correct answer
  • Goal: Get as close to 21 total points as possible without going over
  • Bust Rule: If you reach 22+ points, you automatically lose that round
  • Winner: Player closest to 21 points without busting wins

BlackJack Scoring

How points are calculated:

  • Points = |Your Answer - Correct Answer|
  • Lower points are better
  • Goal: Get close to 21 total points
  • 22+ total points = BUST (elimination)
Example:
Question: "How many legs does a spider have?"
Correct Answer: 8
Player says 6 → 2 points
Player says 10 → 2 points
Player says 8 → 0 points
